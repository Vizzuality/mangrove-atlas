# GLOBAL INTERTIDAL WETLAND CHANGE

Global Intertidal Change is a long-running collaborative project to develop high-resolution maps of Earth's intertidal environments and their recent change. Our overarching aim is to develop free, high-quality spatial data products that support the conservation and management of the world's rapidly changing coastal ecosystems.
